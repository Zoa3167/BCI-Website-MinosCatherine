# Excluded Image Sources

The following sources may look realistic but should not be automatically used in your project unless you separately verify licensing or obtain permission:

- Getty Images / iStock EEG photos
- Wired article photos of Utah arrays
- Medium blog images
- ResearchGate figure images, unless pulled from the original open-access publication with clear license
- Company product pages such as Blackrock Neurotech, Neuralink, etc., unless permission/license is clear

Use the assets in `assets_manifest.json` first.
